Lasagna

Description

Homemade Lasagna is a classic that every cook should have in their rotation. Tender sheets of pasta, a cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce make the perfect dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage.
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef.
  • ½ cup minced onion.
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed.
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes.
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste.
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce.
  • ½ cup water.

Steps

  1. Cheese Filling Mix the ricotta and cheeses (per recipe below) with an egg in a bowl, and set aside.
  2. Meat Sauce Brown the onion, garlic, and meat in a pot on the stovetop.
  3. Layer Together Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with noodles and bake until bubbling.
